About Bar Sitting

Connecting Galway's vibrant coffee culture

Galway is known for its rich coffee culture and welcoming community. Bar Sitting was born from the need to connect the city's talented baristas with cafés that value quality and flexibility.

Our platform facilitates these connections, creating opportunities for everyone: cafés get the coverage they need, baristas find flexible work, and Galway's coffee community grows stronger.
